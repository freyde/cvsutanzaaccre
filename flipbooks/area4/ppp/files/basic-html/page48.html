                       Section 4. SEAL - The seal (or logo) used by CIRCLE OF POTENCIAL EDUCATORS is
                       round shaped with blue sky background that represent the Teacher Education programs –
                       The Bachelor of Secondary Education and Bachelor of Elementary Education. Encrypted
                       at  the  upper  portion  of  the  circle  is  the  ―Cavite  State  University‖.  This  means  that  the
                       program was patterned from the said prestigious university. Encrypted at the bottom part
                       of the circle is name of the campus where the organization is located – Tanza Campus.

                       Encrypted  at  the  center  is  the  word  ‗‗CoPE‖  which  is  abbreviation  of  the  organization.
                       Under it is the year ―2012‖ which determined the year   when  the  year  the  organization
                       was organized.

                       The following symbolisms were used in the logo:
                           4.1.  Book - symbolizes knowledge and wisdom that can be gained by any member.
                           4.2.  Torch - symbolizes the academic program, BSE and BEE which        are  directly
                                under the said organization;
                           4.3.  Flame - designed as CvSU, the adopted acronym of the Cavite State University,
                                symbolizes  the  commitment  of  the  organization  toward  the    university  mission
                                and vision.
                           4.4.  Gear - located below the book, symbolizes the driving force of the organization in
                                the pursuant of its objective, that is, ‖making a difference‖; and
                           4.5.  Medal  –  symbolized  excellence  in  every  undertakings  that  the  organization  is
                                being  committed to.


                       ARTICLE III. MEMBERSHIP

                       Section 1. CIRCLE OF POTENCIAL EDUCATORS shall accept member only if they are;
                       (1)  bonafide  student  of  CAVITE  STATE  UNIVERSITY-Tanza  Campus  (CvSU-TC);  (2)
                       currently  taking  up  Bachelor  of  Secondary  Education  and  Bachelor  of  Elementary
                       Education.

                           1.1.  All students who are taking up Bachelor of Secondary Education and Bachelor of
                                Elementary Education are required to join the organization
                       Section 2. All applicants for membership shall be required with the following:

                           2.1.  School ID for the current academic year or semester.
                           2.2.  Fully signed membership form with recent 1x1 ID picture attached on it.
                           2.3.  Monthly membership fee worth Php 10.00

                       Section  3.  Active  Member  in  Good  Standing  -  Active  membership  in  good  standing  is
                       essential  to  entitlement,  and  the  exercise  of  membership‘s  rights  and  privileges  in  the
                       organization. A member of good standing is one whose membership is          active   and
                       not under suspension or expulsion, or has nor revoked, cancelled or terminated.
